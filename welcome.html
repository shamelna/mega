🔍 Assessment Dimensions

Our comprehensive evaluation covers the 7 essential dimensions of LEAN maturity

👥 Leadership & Culture

Management commitment, employee engagement, and continuous improvement mindset throughout the organisation.

⚙️ Process Management

Standardised work, process optimisation, and systematic approach to operations and workflows.

🏆 Quality & Continuous Improvement

Quality systems, improvement cycles, problem-solving methodologies, and performance measurement.

🎯 Customer Focus

Customer value, satisfaction, feedback systems, and customer-centric approach to operations.

🔧 Operational Excellence

Workplace organisation, visual management, equipment effectiveness, and operational efficiency.

📚 About MEGA

MEGA Continuous Improvement Training & Coaching Model

The MEGA (Mid Ulster Enterprises Group) Continuous Improvement Training and Coaching Model is a comprehensive framework designed to help organisations achieve operational excellence through LEAN principles. Our proven approach combines assessment, training, and ongoing coaching to ensure sustainable improvement.

Our model supports organisations at every stage of their continuous improvement journey — from initial assessment through implementation and beyond. The LEAN Maturity Assessment serves as the foundational tool that informs your personalised coaching programme and improvement roadmap.

Through our structured approach, organisations develop the capabilities, culture, and systems needed for long-term operational excellence and continuous improvement success.
